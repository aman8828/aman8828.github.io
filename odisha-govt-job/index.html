Odisha Govt Job 2025

Odisha Govt Job opportunities are increasing every year with new vacancies for teaching, police, health, banking, and administrative sectors. Aspirants looking for secure and respected careers can apply online for OPSC, OSSC, OSSSC, and other department jobs. These Odisha govt job notifications help candidates build a stable future with government benefits and job security.

Overview of Odisha Govt Job

The Government of Odisha releases thousands of vacancies every year through various recruitment boards such as OPSC, OSSC, OSSSC, and the Education & Health Departments. These Odisha govt job openings are available for candidates with qualifications ranging from 10th pass to postgraduate degrees.

From clerk and assistant roles to high-level officer posts, Odisha provides opportunities for all levels of aspirants.

Popular Departments Offering Odisha Govt Job

  • Odisha Public Service Commission (OPSC): Group-A and Group-B officer posts
  • Odisha Staff Selection Commission (OSSC): Assistants, clerks, auditors
  • OSSSC: Rural and field-level jobs like RI, ARI, AMIN
  • Education Department: Teacher and lecturer posts
  • Odisha Police Recruitment Board: Constable, SI, and battalion posts
  • Health Department: Nurse, ANM, GNM, medical posts
  • Banking Sector: Clerk and officer jobs in nationalized banks
  • WCD / Anganwadi: Worker and supervisor posts

These departments provide stable careers with excellent benefits and growth opportunities.

Eligibility Criteria

The eligibility requirements for Odisha govt job depend on the position and department. Common criteria include:

  • Applicant must be a permanent resident of Odisha
  • Minimum qualification: 10th / 12th / Graduate / PG
  • Age limit: 18 to 38 years (relaxations available)
  • Computer knowledge (for administrative posts)
  • Physical standards for Police and Fire Services

How to Apply for Odisha Govt Job

Applicants can apply online through official recruitment portals:

  • OPSC Official Website
  • OSSC Official Website
  • OSSSC Official Website

Steps to apply online:

  • Visit the respective official website
  • Check the latest recruitment notification
  • Register using your mobile number and email
  • Fill the application form
  • Upload documents, photo, and signature
  • Submit the form and download the confirmation

Some rural-level jobs may accept offline applications through local government offices.

Benefits of Odisha Govt Job

Government jobs in Odisha provide several benefits such as:

  • Job security and long-term stability
  • Attractive salary and allowances
  • Pension and retirement benefits
  • Maternity, medical, and travel benefits
  • Career growth with promotions
  • Work-life balance and social respect
