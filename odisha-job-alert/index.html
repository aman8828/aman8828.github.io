Eligibility Criteria

Every Odisha govt job has its own eligibility rules, but the general requirements include:

  • Applicant must be a resident of Odisha
  • Educational qualification: 10th / 12th / Diploma / Graduate / Postgraduate
  • Age limit usually 18–38 years (relaxation available)
  • Computer skills needed for clerical posts
  • Physical fitness for police and fire service jobs

How to Apply for Odisha Govt Jobs

Applicants can easily apply online through the official recruitment websites:

  • OPSC Official Website
  • OSSC Official Website
  • OSSSC Official Website

General steps to apply:

  • Visit the respective recruitment portal
  • Find the latest job alert notification
  • Register using your mobile number and email
  • Fill the detailed application form
  • Upload documents, photo, and signature
  • Submit the form and print the confirmation page

Some rural-level job alerts may also allow offline application through local offices.
