Female Govt Jobs in Odisha

Female Govt Jobs in Odisha are one of the best opportunities for women looking for secure and respected careers in 2025. The Odisha government offers a variety of posts in teaching, health, banking, and administration. These female govt jobs in Odisha empower women with stable income, job security, and professional growth.

Overview

The state of Odisha provides numerous female govt jobs in Odisha every year through the Odisha Staff Selection Commission (OSSC), Odisha Public Service Commission (OPSC), and Women & Child Development Department. These job openings encourage women to participate actively in public service and administration.

From entry-level clerical roles to high-level officer posts, female govt jobs in Odisha cover all educational and professional levels.

Popular Sectors Offering Female Govt Jobs in Odisha

  • Teaching and Education: Teachers, lecturers, and principals under OPSC and Education Department.
  • Healthcare: Nurse, ANM, GNM, pharmacist, and doctor posts in government hospitals.
  • Police and Defense: Women constables, sub-inspectors, and jail wardens under Odisha Police Recruitment.
  • Administrative Services: OAS, OCS, and clerical roles under various government offices.
  • Banking and Finance: Clerk, officer, and assistant posts for women in nationalized banks.
  • Social Welfare and Anganwadi: Supervisor and helper positions in WCD Odisha.

These female govt jobs in Odisha provide equal pay, maternity benefits, and secure work environments.

Eligibility Criteria

The eligibility requirements for female govt jobs in Odisha vary depending on the position. Common criteria include:

  • Odisha domicile certificate
  • Minimum qualification: 10th, 12th, Graduate, or Postgraduate
  • Age limit: 18 to 35 years (relaxations for SC/ST/OBC categories)
  • Basic computer knowledge (for administrative posts)
  • Physical fitness for police and defense jobs

Women candidates receive additional benefits such as age relaxation and reserved quotas in various recruitment processes.

Application Process for Female Govt Jobs in Odisha

Applying for female govt jobs in Odisha is simple and can be done online through official portals:

  • Visit OPSC or OSSC website
  • Check the latest notifications for women job openings
  • Register with your email and mobile number
  • Fill the application form and upload required documents
  • Submit and download your confirmation slip

Offline applications are also accepted for specific rural posts such as Anganwadi and health worker positions.
