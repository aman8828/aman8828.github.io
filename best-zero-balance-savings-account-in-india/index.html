What is a Zero Balance Savings Account?

A zero balance savings account is a type of bank account where you are not required to maintain any minimum balance. Unlike regular savings accounts, there are no penalties for keeping your account balance at zero.

These accounts are also known as Basic Savings Bank Deposit Accounts (BSBDA) and are promoted by banks to encourage financial inclusion in India.

  • No minimum balance requirement
  • No penalty charges
  • Free debit card (in most cases)
  • Access to mobile and internet banking

One of the biggest advantages is that you can operate your account freely without worrying about penalties, which are common in regular savings accounts. 0
