Odisha Government Job 2025

Odisha Government Job opportunities are increasing with new recruitment notifications in administrative, police, teaching, and health sectors. Overview of Odisha Government Job

The Odisha government releases thousands of job notifications every year across various departments. These include Group-A officer posts, clerical jobs, district-level positions, and field jobs. Odisha government jobs offer job security, good salary, pension, and growth opportunities—making them a preferred choice among youth.

All major Odisha government job notifications are published on OPSC, OSSC, and OSSSC websites.

Eligibility for Odisha Government Job

Eligibility requirements vary by post, but common criteria include:

  • Applicant must be a permanent resident of Odisha
  • Educational qualification: 10th / 12th / ITI / Diploma / Graduate / PG
  • Age limit usually 18–38 years (relaxation for SC/ST/OBC)
  • Computer proficiency for clerical jobs
  • Physical fitness for police and fire services

How to Apply for Odisha Government Jobs

Applications for Odisha government job vacancies can be submitted online:

  • OPSC Official Website
  • OSSC Official Website
  • OSSSC Official Website

General steps to apply:

  • Visit the official recruitment portal
  • Open the latest notification
  • Complete the online registration
  • Fill in the application details
  • Upload documents and signature
  • Submit and download the acknowledgment slip

Benefits of Odisha Government Job

  • Job security and long-term career stability
  • Good salary, allowances, and bonuses
  • Pension and retirement benefits
  • Maternity and medical allowances
  • Work-life balance and fixed working hours
  • Promotions and training opportunities

Candidates get a respectful and stable career in public service.
